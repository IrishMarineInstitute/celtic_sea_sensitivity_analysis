All available layers were summed and then converted to a binary (presence/absence) raster. Layers included were from the Marine Institute ICeco (Irish Coastal Ecosystem) survey (for Pennatula phosphorea), JNCC Marine Recorder (of Funiculina quadrangularis, Pennatula phosphorea, Virgularia mirabilis), Marine Institute benthic survey records (of Virgularia mirabilis), OBIS records (of Funiculina quadrangularis, Pennatula phosphorea, Virgularia mirabilis), ODIMS and OSPAR data (for sea pens and burrowing megafauna), and the Marine Institute underwater TV survey (sea pens).
